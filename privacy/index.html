Privacy

Clear about what the app sends and what it keeps.

The current version of Wayline builds itineraries from the trip details you enter. It does not include accounts or built-in saved trip history.

Trip request

What you enter

Origin, destination, driving days, fuel interval, and brand preferences are sent to the planner backend.

Processing

What the backend does

The backend calls Google Routes API and Places API, applies the trip-planning logic, and returns the result to you.

Exports

What you keep

PDF, text, and JSON exports are generated for you to save locally after the itinerary is built.
