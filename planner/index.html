Before you go

The route is easy. The stopping plan is the real work.

Wayline handles the parts that usually end up scattered across tabs: splitting the drive into sensible days, keeping fuel stops close to the route, and finding overnight areas with familiar hotel options and easy highway access.

  • Uses live Google Maps route and place data.
  • Prefers practical stop quality over mathematically perfect spacing.
  • Exports the finished trip to Maps, PDF, text, and JSON.
